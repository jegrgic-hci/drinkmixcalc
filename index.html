Glucose:fructose ratio

Your gut uses two separate intestinal transporters simultaneously — SGLT-1 for glucose and GLUT-5 for fructose. Combining both lets you absorb significantly more carbs per hour than glucose alone. Select the ratio that matches your experience level and how well you've trained your gut.

Bottle size

Your bottle size determines the maximum carb concentration. High-osmolality sources (maltodextrin, dextrose, trehalose) should stay below 10% concentration to avoid GI distress — so a larger bottle allows a higher total energy intake.
